I'm a technical game designer and gameplay programmer based in Seattle. I thrive on rapid iteration — subtle changes make a huge difference, and a lot of design happens with very simple prototypes.

When I join a project, my first question is: "How can I make this happen?"

Making games solo is satisfying, but the collaborative process is what makes this a passion. Working with a team toward a shared goal is where I do my best work.

When I'm not making games, I'm playing them. I gravitate toward unique indie games with simple, elegant designs. Current favorite: UFO 50.
