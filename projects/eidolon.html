Eidolon: Fate of Fools

Designer · Programmer · Live Ops
2024–2025 · Arena Shooter · UE5 · 9-person team

A multiplayer arena shooter designed to emulate the chaotic camaraderie of old-school LAN parties.

I prototyped and iterated on core gameplay over a year, tuning combat feel through frequent playtesting. I built a live telemetry pipeline that captures player data and sends it to PostgreSQL for analysis. I integrated Steam platform features, including match invites and avatars.
